# StudyOS

StudyOS is an all-in-one productivity dashboard built specifically for students.
It helps you manage your academic life in one place — from planning and focus sessions to tracking goals and analyzing performance.

---

## Features

### Dashboard Overview
- Daily progress tracking
- Study streak counter
- Quick access to all tools

### Study Planner
- Plan your study sessions
- Organize subjects and schedules
- Stay structured and consistent

### Pomodoro Focus Timer
- Custom focus and break durations
- Visual progress ring
- Session tracking

### Goals Tracker
- Set academic goals
- Track completion status
- Stay motivated

### Notes Manager
- Quick note creation
- Clean and distraction-free layout
- Local saving

### Study Journal
- Reflect on your daily progress
- Build productive habits

### Papers & Exams Tracker
- Track upcoming exams
- Manage deadlines
- Stay ahead of schedule

### Performance Analyzer
- Review productivity trends
- Improve time management
- Monitor study consistency

---

## Why StudyOS?

Students often juggle:
- Multiple subjects
- Deadlines
- Exams
- Focus issues
- Productivity challenges

StudyOS brings everything together into one clean, minimal, and distraction-free interface.
No clutter. No unnecessary complexity.
Just productivity.
